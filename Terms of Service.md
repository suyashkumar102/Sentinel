## 2. Description of Service
Sentinel is an automated, server-side tool designed to assist subreddit moderators in detecting escalating behavioral patterns among community contributors. The App aggregates public, moderator-visible actions (such as posts, comments, warnings, removals, and reports) to generate a multi-dimensional trajectory score. 

Sentinel operates entirely inside the Reddit Devvit sandboxed server environment and does not make external network requests or integrate third-party APIs.
